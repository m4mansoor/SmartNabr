Overview

Intelligent Water
Monitoring for Industry

SmartNabr is an AI-powered, IoT-enabled smart water monitoring solution designed for industrial and commercial facilities across Saudi Arabia. It delivers real-time visibility into water consumption, tank levels, flow rates, and anomalies — keeping your operations efficient, compliant, and waste-free.

Water Waste is a
Costly Silent Problem

Water is scarce and expensive in KSA — every liter wasted directly impacts operational costs.

Hidden leaks go unnoticed for days or weeks, causing massive financial and infrastructural losses.

👷

Manual Monitoring

Relying on manual checks is labor-intensive, unreliable, and leaves critical gaps in visibility.
